Details to Consider When Choosing an Education Loan

  • Loan Amount: The maximum amount you can borrow based on the bank's policy.
  • Interest Rate: The percentage charged by the bank, which can be fixed or floating.
  • Repayment Tenure: The period over which you repay the loan (typically 15 years).
  • Processing Fees: Whether the bank charges an application or processing fee.
  • Prepayment Penalty: Any charges if you pay off the loan before the term ends.
